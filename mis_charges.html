NOTE ON ASSUMPTIONS Since virtually all individuals booked have multiple charges, the following charges are inlcuded along with marijuana offenses. The working assumption for the above analysis is that these charges do not require an individual to be booked and that the marijuana charges were the precipitating factor. However given the subjective nature of police work and that each incident is unique, the above analysis should be taken as an estimation of general trends and not a rigorous statement of fact.
